**Procedural SFX Companion** is the free, open-source, cross-platform companion application designed to work seamlessly alongside the **Procedural SFX & Music Plugin** for Unreal Engine 5.

It delivers lightning-fast graph searching and advanced editing features to significantly enhance your procedural audio design workflow directly inside Unreal Engine.

> 🌐 **Cross-Platform Note:** The project supports cross-platform compatibility (Windows, macOS, and Linux). However, during the current phase, build binaries and active testing are **exclusively focused on Windows**.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Cross-Platform & Open Source:** Completely free to use, modify, and distribute. Built to run flawlessly across Windows, macOS, and Linux.
* **Advanced Graph Searching & Editing:** Effortlessly navigate, query, and modify your complex procedural audio graphs.
* **The Central Hub:** Your central destination for grabbing the latest updates, browsing comprehensive documentation, accessing community support, and watching video tutorials.
* **And more to come...** We are actively developing this companion app, with new features, and new Unreal Engine integration planned for future updates.

---

## 📦 Installation

1. Head over to the [Releases](https://github.com/Hikaru-sin/ProceduralSFXCompanion/releases) page.
2. Download the latest archive compatible with your Operating System.
3. Extract and use the application.

---

## 🛠️ Requirements
* **Engine:** Unreal Engine 5.6-5.8
* **Plugin:** [Procedural SFX & Music Plugin](https://www.fab.com/listings/738ae468-a616-4051-ae03-853b53bc2de4) installed in your Unreal Editor 5 or project.
